Frequently Asked Questions

How do you verify land titles?

We work with title companies and use public records to verify ownership, liens, and encumbrances. For an additional fee, we can provide a preliminary title report.

What fees do you charge?

Listing is free for sellers. We charge a 3% commission upon successful sale. Buyers pay no fees. Additional services like title verification or escrow have separate fees.

Can I buy land from another state?

Yes! That's the advantage of digital transactions. All documentation can be handled remotely. We recommend a site visit before purchase, but it's not required for the transaction.

How long does the process take?

From initial contact to closing typically takes 30-45 days, depending on due diligence and financing. Cash transactions can close in as little as 2 weeks.
