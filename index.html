Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about buying and selling land

How do you verify land titles?

We work with certified title companies and access public records to verify ownership, liens, and encumbrances. Every listing undergoes a preliminary title check before going live.

Can I buy land from another municipal?

Yes! That's the advantage of digital transactions. All documentation can be handled remotely. We recommend a site visit before purchase, but it's not required for the transaction.

What fees do you charge?

Listing is free for sellers. We charge a 3% commission upon successful sale. Buyers pay no fees. Additional services like enhanced title verification or escrow have separate transparent fees.

How long does the process take?

From initial contact to closing typically takes 30-45 days, depending on due diligence and financing. Cash transactions can close in as little as 2 weeks with our expedited process.
