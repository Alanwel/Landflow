Important Tips for Buying Land in Uganda

✓ Do These:

  • Verify land at URSB (Uganda Registration Services Bureau)
  • Get consent from family for Mailo land transfers
  • Use a lawyer for agreement drafting
  • Visit physical site before payment

✗ Avoid These:

  • Don't buy without proper documentation
  • Avoid buying disputed family land
  • Don't pay full amount before verification
  • Avoid verbal agreements only
